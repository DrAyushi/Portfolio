Project 01  ·  Flagship

KDAH Smart Billing Compliance System — IRDAI Non-Payables

Insurance companies deduct items from hospital bills after the patient has left. The hospital then chases the patient for money that should have been collected at discharge. This system identifies, before discharge, exactly which items each insurer will never pay — and automatically routes those to the patient bill so the hospital collects upfront, every time.

Project 02  ·  Flagship

Hospital Revenue Protection System (HRPS)

Hospitals lose crores silently: corporate companies that underpay after discharge, manual follow-ups falling through the cracks, insurance deductions nobody disputes. HRPS is an intelligent 3-module Python system that closes all three leaks before and after patient discharge.

Module 1 — Corporate Risk Scorer

Before discharge, scores each employer on 5 parameters: payment ratio, average delay, outstanding dues, dispute count, dispute rate. Score 70+ means collect advance before the patient leaves.

Module 2 — Automated Follow-up Tracker

After discharge, every pending bill triggers escalating reminders: Day 7 email, Day 15 senior alert, Day 30 CFO notification, Day 45 legal review. Finance team gets a daily action list.

Module 3 — Deduction Recovery Engine

Auto-detects every insurance shortfall, categorises the reason, generates a dispute letter, and tracks recovery status. ₹75L+ in additional revenue identified as recoverable.
